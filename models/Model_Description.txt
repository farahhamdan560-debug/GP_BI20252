model description:
In this project, advanced analytical and data models were implemented 
directly within the Business Intelligence environment to support decision-making:

1. Predictive Modeling (Time Series Forecasting):
   - Purpose: Applied to forecast future product demand and optimal stock levels based on historical sales trends.
   - Technology: Power BI Built-in Forecasting Engine.

2. Descriptive Modeling (Cluster Analysis):
   - Purpose: Used to segment and classify inventory items into distinct groups based on their movement patterns and sales behavior.
   - Technology: Power BI Clustering Segmentation.
3. Data Preparation & ETL Workflow:
   - Purpose: Performed advanced data cleaning, preparation, and transformation pipelines before feeding the data into the system.
   - Technology: KNIME Analytics Platform.

* Note: 
All analytical models and visualization layers are fully integrated 
and saved within the main Power BI dashboard file (.pbix).
